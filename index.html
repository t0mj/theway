About Us

Our Mission

We exist to do three things: Love Upward, Inward, and Outward

Upward

Love God completely and keep him first in everything that is done

Inward

Love each other as God loves us by encouraging and helping our brothers and sisters in the church

Outward

Love the lost and hurting with the same love God shows us; spreading this love to the local community and beyond

Statement of Faith

We believe

in the inerrancy of Scripture, that the Bible, Old and...

in the inerrancy of Scripture, that the Bible, Old and Testaments, is the inspired, infallible Word of God, a complete and final written revelation of God. (Isaiah 28:13; Nehemiah 8:8; John 17:17; II Timothy 3:16-17; Hebrews 4:12; I Peter 1:23-25; II Peter 1:3-4, 21).

in one personal, and holy God, the creator of all, Who...

in one personal, and holy God, the creator of all, Who eternal, and Who manifests Himself in three separate persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit. (Genesis 1:1; Deuteronomy 6:4; Isaiah 44:8, 48:16; Matthew 28:19-20; John 10:30; Hebrews 1:3)

that Jesus Christ, though fully God, became a man...

that Jesus Christ, though fully God, became a man for suffering of death, that He is the promised Messiah, born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, provided for the atonement of our sins by His death on the Cross, was resurrected by the power of the Holy Spirit, ascended back to the right hand of God the Father, and ever lives to make intercession for us. After Jesus ascended to Heaven, He poured out His Holy Spirit on the believers in Jerusalem, enabling them to fulfill His command to preach the Gospel to the entire world, an obligation shared by all believers today. (Isaiah 7:14; Micah 5:2; Matthew 1:23; Mark 16:19; Luke 1:34-35; John 1:1-2, 8:58,11:25; I Corinthians 15:3-4; I Timothy 3:16; Hebrews 1:8; I John 1:2; Revelation 1:8)

that all people are by nature separated from God...

that all people are by nature separated from God responsible for their own sin, but that salvation, redemption, and forgiveness are freely offered to all by the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ. When a person repents of sin and accepts Jesus Christ as personal Savior and Lord, trusting Him to save, that person is immediately born again and sealed by the Holy Spirit, all his/her sins are forgiven, and that person becomes a child of God. (Matthew 11:28; John 3:16; Acts 3:19, 20:21; Romans 3:23; Ephesians 2:1-3, 8-9; I John 2:2; Revelation 22:17)
